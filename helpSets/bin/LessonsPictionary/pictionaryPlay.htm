Executing Pictionary Lessons

When you execute a Pictionary lesson, you will see a series of pictures shown on the frame. If there are more than can fit, a scroll bar will enable you to view all of them. When you click on one of the pictures, you will see the gloss (first language) translation, indigenous spelling, and additional descriptive information attached to the picture.

On the bottom of your display you will see a row of buttons that are standard across all lesson types. For more information Click Here. For many lesson types, clicking on the middle button (), cause a pop up menu to appear, which have options that are unique to each lesson type. Pictionary lessons have no special options, so clicking on this button will simply beep.
