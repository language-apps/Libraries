Executing Story Book Lessons

When you execute a Story Book, you will see a picture at the top that relates to the audio story contained in the lesson. You will also see a series of control buttons near the bottom of the picture. These operate in accordance with the following list.


ReplayThis button rewinds the recording at the same speed at which it played. This is useful if you want to back up the recording to rehear a word or a phrase.
PauseThis button pauses the recording at the current place. It is used in connection with the rewind button.
StopThis button stops the recording and goes back to the beginning. This is useful if you want to replay the recording from the beginning.
PlayThe button starts the playing of the audio story. As it plays the word in the story that is playing will highlight. Note though, that this is only a rough estimate because setup mode did not require you to select the exact spot where the words appeared in the sound wave.
